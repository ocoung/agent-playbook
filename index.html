❌ Bad Cron Prompt
"Check the stock market and tell me what's good"

🤔 Which market? How many stocks? What's "good"? How long should the response be?
✅ Good Cron Prompt
"Scan pre-market data and suggest 3 mid-to-large cap stocks based on volume, gap-ups, and news catalysts. For each: ticker, price, catalyst, risk note. Under 200 words."
📘 The Watchdog Pattern
Monitor something and ONLY alert when it matters. Silence when things are fine. Instant ping when they're not. Like a security camera that only records when something moves.
💡 The Escape Hatch Trick
Add to every cron prompt: "If nothing needs attention, respond HEARTBEAT_OK." Without this, your agent spams you with "Everything's fine! 😊" at 6 AM. Every. Single. Day.
